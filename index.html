Welcome

We study the molecular basis of photosynthesis and engineer new traits using synthetic biology. Our work focuses on improving plant performance, carbon assimilation, and stress tolerance through innovative genomic and biochemical approaches.

Research Areas

Photosynthetic Efficiency

Understanding C3, C4, and CAM photosynthetic pathways and their evolution.

Synthetic Biology

Genetic circuits, metabolic engineering, and chassis optimization in plants.

Chloroplast Engineering

Using plastid transformation to reprogram photosynthetic metabolism.

Gene Regulation

Promoter analysis and control of photosynthesis-related gene expression.
