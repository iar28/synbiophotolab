About us

The Synthetic biology & Photosynthesis group at CRAG works on modifying plant photosynthetic capacity to improve crop productivity under heat and drought stress. We study how rearrangements of gene regulatory networks shaped the evolution of carbon-concentrating mechanisms like C4 and CAM, and we use genomics, phenomics, and synthetic biology tools to engineer new photosynthetic pathways. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
